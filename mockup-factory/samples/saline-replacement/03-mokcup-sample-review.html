Guidance for Lipemic Interference
Step 1: Confirm Lipemia with Chemistry Results

Review Chemistry panel for Cholesterol and Triglycerides. Look for elevated values and potential 'LIH' (Lipemia Index) flags indicating severe lipemia.

Step 2: Perform Saline Replacement (if Lipemia Confirmed)
  1. Centrifuge an aliquot of the original sample.
  2. Carefully remove the lipemic plasma.
  3. Add an equal volume of normal saline (0.9% NaCl) to the packed red cells.
  4. Gently resuspend the cells.
  5. Re-run the prepared sample on the DxH800 instrument.
  6. Manually enter or verify the corrected hematology results.
