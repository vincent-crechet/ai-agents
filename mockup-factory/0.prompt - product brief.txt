This document describes a Clinical Laboratory Information Technology (CIT) middleware product designed for large Integrated Delivery Networks (IDN) with multiple laboratory sites.

Target Users (Personas):
- Lab Technician: Performs daily sample processing and result review tasks
- CIT Expert: Configures and manages the middleware system
- Lab Director: Oversees laboratory operations and makes strategic decisions
- Quality Manager: Ensures compliance and quality standards are met
- IT Director: Manages IT infrastructure and system reliability
- C-Suite: Reviews high-level performance metrics and business outcomes

Applications:

Clinical Diagnostics Applications

Application 1: Sample Workflow Management
Intelligently automates and optimizes sample processing with configurable processing rules, automated workflows, clinical quality monitoring, and intelligent analyzer connectivity.

Application 2: Sample Review Management
Provides integrated tools for reviewing results with unified patient views, streamlined critical result reporting, proactive sample and task management, and efficient multi-sample batch actions.

Application 3: Clinical Workflow Optimization
Delivers intelligent guidance for diagnostic interpretations, helps identify root causes of errors, proactively adapts to best practices, and enhances overall lab accuracy with delta check guidance and auto-verification recommendations.

Application 4: Multi-Site Sample Routing Management
Provides complete visibility and intelligent, dynamic routing for samples across an IDN with end-to-end sample logistics tracking and guided multi-site sample transfer based on test menu and instrument status.

Lab Operations Applications

Application 5: QC Management
Optimizes QC management with efficient processing and flexible reporting including peer review of QC values, flexible report creation, support for multiple QC protocols, QC by pack, and rule optimization guidance.

Application 6: Inventory Management and Billing
Enhances lab efficiency by automating reagent management, optimizing inventory across sites, tracking lot and expiry usage, and providing insights on test usage including automated replenishment, inventory tracking across multiple sites, and metering solutions.

Application 7: Real-Time Lab Status
Maximizes lab efficiency and minimizes downtime by allowing users to monitor, manage, and interact with lab systems in real time, providing actionable analytics, digitizing daily routines, and anticipating system issues.

Application 8: Lab Performance Optimization
Enriches data-driven decision making with the ability to drill down into raw data, leverage AI/ML for identifying improvement areas, manage resources efficiently, visualize instrument menu usage, and customize reports tailored to different user roles.

Application 9: Efficient Service Management
Enables proactive monitoring and alert visualization to detect and resolve issues before they affect operations with remote access capabilities, clear maintenance scheduling, and PM status visualization for the entire instrument network.

Compliance/Audit Applications

Application 10: Results Compliance
Provides tools to prove samples are processed properly and results validated and released on time, listing all events related to samples and results with comprehensive audit trails for sample traceability, device communication, result handling, and sample-based errors.

Application 11: QC Compliance
Provides tools to prove QC compliance, reduce risk during inspections, standardize QC practices across labs, and quickly trace issues with audit trails for QC status, QC review, instrument events, and peer review across sites.

Application 12: User Operations Compliance
Ensures comprehensive auditing of PHI access and training/certification management including user activity tracking, configuration change logging, and training and certification management with system access restrictions based on certifications.

Infrastructure Applications

Application 13: Simple Install/Update Process
Enables quick, easy deployment and maintenance with minimal hands-on time, simple migration capabilities, easy dictionary construction, customer-driven software updates with minimal downtime, integrated test systems, and update history tracking.

Application 14: Highly Reliable System
Ensures uninterrupted lab operations through flexible deployment options (customer IT or cloud), disaster recovery without data loss, high availability capabilities, and remote service including telemetry and remote monitoring.

Application 15: Core Connectivity
Ensures seamless integration and efficient data exchange with LIS connection, connection to analyzers and instruments, third-party instrument connectivity, and third-party middleware integration.
